Our Story

Founded in 2025, Yunoxa began as a bold idea between university friends who believed Sri Lankan consumers deserved more from e-commerce. We noticed a gap between global shopping experiences and what was available locally.

What started as a small platform with just 50 sellers has grown into Sri Lanka's most innovative marketplace, connecting thousands of buyers with unique products and experiences.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of Sri Lanka's digital commerce revolution, constantly pushing boundaries to deliver "Tomorrow's Vibes, Today."
